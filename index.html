About Me

Something You Should Know...

I have learned the majority of my programming languages by myself with online resources. I am currently a sophomore enrolled in high school. Even all the homework that is loaded on to me, I will always find time to kick back and type away slews of code on my laptop. So why do I code?

I'm not getting a job soon. Heck, I'm not even in college! So why do I code? I do it simply for that moment of satisfaction seeing the product come together. My eyes will wander to each corner of the screen noticing each and every nuance and realizing I had made all of it from scratch!

There's alot of time and dedication that goes into being able to create a beautiful program. I have to make sure that everything I do when I program, I am doing out of pure interest. That's why I have such a diverse spectrum of languages that I'm proficient in. I spend most of my time now working on improving my Java, C, and Python skills to better compete in coding competitions. I find being challenged with tricky problems and coming up with bizarre yet correct answers my favorite part of it all!
